Rosemary Chicken Legs

Roasted chicken legs with succulent hits of lemon and rosemary! A perfectly clean and easy main dish for dinner!

Ingredients

  • Raw chicken legs.
  • Large white onion.
  • Lemon
  • Garlic cloves.
  • Fresh rosemary.
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil.

Steps

  1. Begin by chopping up the onion into large pieces about 1/2 in. thick.
  2. Finely dice about two to three garlic cloves.
  3. Wrap your pan or cooking tray with aluminum foil.
  4. Apply a light layer of oil and then the pieces of onion and garlic.
  5. Place the chicken legs on top of the onion and garlic.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the chicken and place slices lemon wheels and rosemary on top.
  7. Add any leftover garlic or onion between the chicken legs.
  8. Prehat oven (or toaster) to 450.
  9. Place tray of chicken into oven(or toaster oven, leaving atleast an inch from the top).
  10. Drop temp to 400 and cook for 25 minutes or until internal temp of chicken is atleast 185.
  11. Remove and let rest for about 15-20 minutes. Enjoy!
