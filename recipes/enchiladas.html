Enchiladas

Delicous enchiladas topped with cheese, smothered in red sauce and filled with seasoned beef, onions, potatoes, and diced pickles. An American twist on the traditional dish!

Ingredients

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Beef
  • Red Enchilada Sauce
  • Taco seasoning of choice
  • Potatoes of choice
  • Large white onion
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Begin by dicing the onion up into 1/4 in. pieces.
  2. Chop the poatoes into tiny squares, about 1/8 in.
  3. Heat some oil in a large deep frying pan.
  4. Add the potatoes and cover, stirring frequently.
  5. Once the potatoes begin to turn transluscent, add the onion.
  6. Now add the beef and taco seasoning.
  7. Stir and cook.
  8. While cooking, cut up the pickles into pieces similar in size to the potatoes.
  9. Add the pickles about halfway through the beef cook.
  10. Cook until there is only a little moisture left.
  11. In a separate bowl add the enchilada sauce.
  12. Dip a tortila into the enchilada sauce, liberally soaking both sides.
  13. Now, on a separate plate, add a scoop or two of the beef an roll. Be sure to tuck the ends in after the first fold.
  14. Fit the enchiladas into an oiled baking tray. It's okay if they are touching and squeezed in a bit.
  15. Cover with more sauce and top with shredded cheese.
  16. Cook in oven on 400 for 20 minutes, or until cheese is melted and enchiladas are throughly heated.
  17. Enjoy!
