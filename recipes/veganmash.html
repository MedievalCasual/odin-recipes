Vegan Mashed Potatoes

A creamy and buttery mashed potato recipe without the cream or butter. This side dish is quick, easy, delicious, and totally vegan!

Ingredients

  • 4-6 large potatoes of choice.
  • Oat or almond milk.
  • A vegan butter of choice (I recommend Myoko)
  • Fresh chives.
  • Salt and pepper.

Steps

  1. First, fill a large pot with water and a bit of salt and bring to a boil.
  2. Peel all of your potatoes.
  3. Chop the potatoes up into cubes and add to boiling water.
  4. Boil until soft, about 20 minutes.
  5. Strain the potates and place in a bowl.
  6. Using a masher, or a fork and "elbow grease", mash the poatoes until all large chunks are gone.
  7. Add peices of butter while the potatoes are still warm and continue to mash.
  8. Then add about quarter cup of the milk alternative slowly as you continue to mash.
  9. Continue mash and add more butter or milk to taste and desired consistency.
  10. Add finely dices chives and stir into the mash.
  11. Enjoy!
